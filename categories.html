Successful people don’t become that way overnight. What most people see at a glance—happiness, wealth, a great career, purpose—is the result of hard work and hustle over time.

We almost get our solution when we understand our problem and frame it in right manner. Our problem sometime is only our believe or thought. So try to differentiate between our believe and the fact.
Push yourself, because no one else is going to do it for you.
